My Favorite Teacher from Each Year

Freshman Year

Ms. Lin's 4th period class was definietly the best class to be in freshman year. Constant experiements, memes, and fun ways to learn was always her thing. Especially in the OG room that she had on the 2nd floor (before she moved to the 4th floor).

Ms. Nam's class was right after Ms. Lin. A classic thing for me to do will be to call Ms. Nam, "Ms. Lin" (due to me having a period before her) and sometimes even vice versa (I promise, it was not on purpose). One of the best things about Ms. Nam was that she would always take the time to answer questions about worldy things. Once, we spent the entirety of class (a 2 period block btw) talking about black representation in Movies.
